Semi-Cryogenic Engine Test Stand at Agnikul Cosmos

Business Motivation:

The company needed a portable semi-cryogenic engine test stand to facilitate testing anywhere with minimal restrictions. This allowed them to perform hot-fire tests in remote locations despite the lack of clear regulations for launch vehicle startups in 2019.

Role and Responsibilities:

I owned the end to end developement of the thrust test stand from ideation till deployment, which included the load-bearing structure, sensor suite, plumbing, instrumentation, and avionics. Used Autodesk Inventor for design and ANSYS for analysis.

Challenges and Solutions:

  • Developed a modular system to accommodate various tests.
  • Designed jet deflectors and other hardware for portable testing.
  • Managed to complete the design cycle in three months by coordinating with multiple teams to define requirements and future test needs.

Impact and Results:

  • Successfully gathered crucial data that contributed to securing funding.
  • Enhanced the understanding of propulsion system performance and subsystem integration.

Service Tool for SparkCharge

Business Motivation:

SpotCharge needed a reliable service tool to address field deployment issues with their V2 product, aiming to improve customer retention and reduce future costs.

Role and Responsibilities:

Led the design and development of a comprehensive service tool that addressed key deployment challenges.

Challenges and Solutions:

  • Created a tool to diagnose and fix field issues, enhancing serviceability.
  • Collaborated with teams to ensure the tool was aligned with the V3 product line's serviceability requirements.

Impact and Results:

  • Improved customer retention by up to 80%.
  • Helped accelerate project timelines by 30%.
  • Contributed to significant future cost savings and influenced design for serviceability in the V3 product line.
